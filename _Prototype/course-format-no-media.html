How long will this product last in protecting a treated area against bed bugs?

Correct!

If you look on the right of the label in the colored/shaded column, you will see in bold letters "PROTECTS FOR UP TO 12 WEEKS." Also at the bottom of this column is a circle with "12" in the middle. Under this, it states "protects up to 12 weeks."
